Harness Chat — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 8, 2026

Harness Chat is a bring-your-own-key AI chat app. We built it to hold as little of your data as possible.

What we store

  • Account: when you sign in with Google or Apple, we store your account identifier and email address (via Supabase authentication).
  • Chats and memories: your conversations and the assistant's memory notes are stored in our database so they sync between your devices. They are visible only to your account.
  • Your OpenRouter API key: stored server-side and used solely to send your messages to the AI models you choose. Model usage is billed to your own OpenRouter account.

What we don't do

  • No ads, no analytics SDKs, no trackers.
  • We never sell or share your data with third parties.
  • Your messages go only to OpenRouter (and the model provider you select) to generate replies, and to DuckDuckGo when you ask for a web search. On-device models run entirely on your phone and send nothing anywhere.

Deleting your data

Deleting a chat removes it from our database. To delete your account and all associated data, email jaden.kwek@gmail.com and we'll remove everything within 30 days.
