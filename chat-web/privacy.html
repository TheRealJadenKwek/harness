Harness Chat — Privacy Policy

Effective July 14, 2026 · Applies to the Harness Chat iOS app, desktop app, and harness-chat-web.vercel.app

Harness Chat is a bring-your-own-key AI chat client. We run no ads, sell no data, and collect nothing beyond what the features below need to work.

What we collect, how, and why

DataHow it's collectedWhy / where it lives
Email addressWhen you sign in with Google or Apple (optional — the app works signed-out)Identifies your account. Stored in our database (Supabase).

Your OpenRouter API keyYou paste it in SettingsPaying for your own AI usage. Stored in Supabase (signed-in) or only on your device (guest mode).
Voice recordingsOnly while you hold the dictation buttonTranscribed to text, then discarded. Never stored.

Third-party AI services — what is sent, and to whom

When you send a message, the content you provide — your message text, any images or documents you attach, relevant conversation history, and saved memory facts — is transmitted to OpenRouter, Inc. (openrouter.ai, United States), which routes it to the AI model provider you selected for that chat (for example OpenAI, Anthropic, Google, xAI, DeepSeek, or Meta, depending on the model you chose). This happens under your own OpenRouter account and API key. The app discloses this and asks for your consent before your first message, and the active model is always shown in the chat toolbar.

OpenRouter's handling of this data is governed by the OpenRouter privacy policy; downstream model providers apply their API data policies, which do not use your content to train models by default. We never use your data for anything except serving your own chats back to you.

If you use web search, the search query the assistant forms is sent to DuckDuckGo. On-device models run entirely on your phone and send nothing anywhere.

What we never do

  • No advertising, analytics SDKs, or trackers of any kind.
  • We never sell your data, and we never share it beyond the AI routing described above.
  • We never train models on your data.
  • Guest mode sends messages directly from your device to OpenRouter — our servers never see them.

Exporting your data

Settings → "Export my data" downloads everything we hold for your account (all chats and memories) as a single JSON file, anytime.

Deleting your account and data

Delete your account any time, in the app: Settings → Delete account. This permanently and immediately removes your account record, email, all synced chats, all memory facts, and your stored OpenRouter key from our database. It cannot be undone. Deleting an individual chat removes it from our database as well.
